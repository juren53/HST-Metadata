Application Record Version      : 4
Credit                          : Harry S. Truman Library
Special Instructions            : Thursday, 06/17/1954
Object Name                     : 2002-200
Caption-Abstract                : James C. Petrillo, President of the American Federation of Musicians, shows off a few dance steps (right) while former President Harry S. Truman, standing before the piano, cheers him on.  They teamed up for three wild choruses of Hail, Hail, the Gang's All Here.
