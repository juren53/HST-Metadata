### Background Information on PortableApps.com

https://portableapps.com/apps

PortableApps.com is a portable software solution allowing you to take your favorite software with you. A fully open source and free platform, it works from any synced cloud folder (DropBox, Google Drive, Box, etc), from your local PC on an internal or external drive, or on any portable storage device (USB flash drive, memory card, portable SSD or hard drive, etc) moved between PCs. With millions of users all over the world, over 500 real open source and freeware apps in the main app store (no shovelware), compatible commercial software, and partners in hardware industry, PortableApps.com is a solution to assist in managing portable applications.
